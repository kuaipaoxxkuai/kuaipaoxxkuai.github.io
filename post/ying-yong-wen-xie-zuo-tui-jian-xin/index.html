I am writing this letter to recommend to you a suitable bookstore,Doctor Bookstore is for the preparation for post-graduate entrance examination. I have been a regular customer of this store.
The reasons for my consideration are as follows. Firstly, the bookstore is near our university, so it is convenient for you to buy books at any time. Secondly, the bookstore’ owner is very nice and has a wide range of knowledge, so if you have any questions about English, you could ask him for help.
I hope that you will find the bookstore worthy of visiting.
Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
